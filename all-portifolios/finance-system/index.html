What this system covers

CRM Overview

Dashboard cards for leads, opportunities, monthly sales, revenue, and conversion metrics give operators a direct view of business performance.

Finance Operations

Accounts, transactions, expenses, expense categories, fuel entries, and settings are modeled as separate operational modules with clear CRUD flows.

Reporting Layer

The project includes monthly report handling and PDF output support, which makes it useful beyond a simple admin panel or starter template.
